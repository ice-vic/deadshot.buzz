Frequently Asked Questions

Is Deadshot free to play?

Yes! Deadshot is completely free to play. There are no subscriptions, hidden fees, or pay-to-win mechanics. Simply load the game in your browser and start playing immediately.

Do I need to create an account to play?

No account is required! You can start playing immediately as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track your progress, save your statistics, and compete on the leaderboards with a persistent profile.

Can I play Deadshot on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Deadshot is fully optimized for mobile devices including smartphones and tablets. The responsive design ensures smooth gameplay and intuitive touch controls across all screen sizes.

What are the system requirements?

Deadshot runs on any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) with JavaScript enabled. No special hardware or software downloads are required. A stable internet connection is recommended for the best multiplayer experience.

How does the scoring system work?

Points are awarded based on eliminations and survival time. Each opponent you eliminate earns you points, and the longer you survive in a match, the more bonus points you accumulate. Your total score determines your position on the leaderboard.

Is there a way to play offline?

An internet connection is required to play Deadshot as it's a multiplayer game that connects you with other players in real-time. The game cannot be played offline, but you can enjoy it on any device with an internet connection.

Are there any tips for beginners?

Start by focusing on movement and positioning. Keep moving to make yourself a harder target. Practice your aim by taking time to line up shots before firing. Use cover when available, and don't be afraid to retreat if you're outnumbered. Most importantly, keep playing - practice is the key to improvement!
